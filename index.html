Funnel context

The funnel matters, but it is not the whole story.

Lead-to-set conversion is a constraint. The bigger operating question is where Britni's team should spend its limited time and where Angi should place the next dollar of budget.

Time allocation finding

High-call leads consume most activity without producing revenue.

Leads with more than 20 calls represented only 32% of lead volume, but consumed 82% of total call activity. They produced one sold job and $0 net revenue.

16,630 calls spent on >20-call leads

Aged unset leads

Stale leads need a lifecycle standard, not unlimited manual follow-up.

As of the June 29 review date, 763 unset leads were at least 30 days old. More than half had fewer than 20 calls, while 332 had already reached 20+ calls.

The answer is a clearer operating model: pursue early, nurture thoughtfully, then close or recycle after a defined threshold.
